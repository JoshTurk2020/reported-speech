Girlfriend Graziela

One day, Jerry and George were having lunch at Monk's Cafe. Jerry said, "I have a new girlfriend, but she has a weird habit."

Jerry said that he had a new girlfriend, but she had a weird habit.

George replied, "What's the habit? It can't be worse than my last girlfriend who collected toenail clippings."

George replied by asking what the habit was, and said it couldn't be worse than his last girlfriend who collected toenail clippings.

Jerry made a disgusted face. "No, nothing that gross," Jerry said. "She just uses a lot of made-up words and catchphrases. It's like she speaks her own language sometimes."

Jerry said it was nothing that gross. He explained that she just used a lot of made-up words and catchphrases, and it was like she spoke her own language sometimes.

George laughed. "I dated a girl who spoke Pig Latin. It got annoying real fast."

George laughed and said he dated a girl who spoke Pig Latin, and it got annoying real fast.

Later, Jerry introduced his girlfriend Graziela to George. Graziela greeted George, "Yo yo yiggity yo, nice to meetcha!"

Graziela greeted George by saying "Yo yo yiggity yo, nice to meetcha!"

George looked confused. He asked Jerry, "Is she having a stroke?"

George asked Jerry if Graziela was having a stroke.

Graziela explained, "That's just how I like to talk. I'm quirky like that!"

Graziela explained that that's just how she liked to talk, and she was quirky like that.

Jerry asked, "See what I mean, George? It's nonstop with the nonsense words."

Jerry asked George whether he saw what he meant and that it was nonstop with the nonsense words.

George suggested to Graziela, "Maybe you should read a dictionary. Learn some real words."

George suggested to Graziela that maybe she should read a dictionary and learn some real words.

Graziela joked back, "You've been single your whole life, haven't you?" She stormed off in anger.

Graziela joked back, asking George whether he had been single his whole life.

Jerry shook his head. "Well, all this joking is making me hungry. Want to order a pizza?"

Jerry shook his head and said that all that joking was making him hungry, before asking (then asked) if they wanted to order a pizza.
